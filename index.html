All About Elephants

Basic Info

Elephants are the largest land animals on Earth, and they're one of the most unique-looking animals, too. With their characteristic long noses, or trunks; large, floppy ears; and wide, thick legs, there is no other animal with a similar physique.vory, which comes from elephant tusks, is considered very valuable. Because of the high price of ivory, poachers illegally kill elephants so that they can take their tusks and sell them. Tens of thousands of elephants are killed each year for their tusks, and as a result, elephant populations have declined rapidly.

Fun Facts about Elephants

  • Elephants are the largest land mammals
  • The leader of the herd is usually the oldest female – she is called the Matriarch
  • A healthy adult elephant can drink up to 60 gallons of water each day – which is the same as about 275 big bottles of coke
  • Elephants are so clever they can peel their own bananas, corn and lots of other food.
